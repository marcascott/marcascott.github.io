  • February 5: DATA SCIENCE LUNCH (food served)
    • Speaker: Ying Lu & Preeti Raghavan
    • Topic: Quantifying Feedforward Control: A Linear Scaling Model for Fingertip Forces and Object Weight
    • Abstract: We fit a linear growth curve to the biomechanical data of grasping in order to understand the relationship between fingertip forces and object weight among healthy subjects. Our results show evidence of feedforward control during the grasping task when healthy subject grasp/lift an object with familiar weights.
    • Time/Loc.: 12:30-1:30pm, Center for Data Science, 726 Broadway, 7th Floor (elevators to far left upon bldg. entry)
